Narrative

In a distant future, the world as we know it has crumbled under the weight of human negligence and greed. The environment has turned hostile, forcing humanity to make a desperate last-ditch effort to survive. They transferred their consciousnesses into advanced cyborg bodies to adapt to the new world. However, this transition brought unforeseen consequences as their once-vibrant bodies turned into prisons, and some lost their very humanity.
